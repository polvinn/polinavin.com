I'm Polina

I work at the intersection of AI, psychology, and product thinking.

I used to design products. Now I work in AI — evaluating models, leading QA projects, and exploring how people and technology shape each other.

My background is a mix of psychology, UX research, product design, and applied AI ethics. I care about clarity, responsibility, and building things that genuinely help people.

What I’m doing now

  • Leading QA and evaluation work on large-scale AI projects
  • Working with researchers and engineers across large AI products
  • Reviewing model outputs, spotting patterns, and improving data quality
  • Analyzing AI safety and policy
  • Exploring AI ethics and sustainable AI
  • Occasionally helping friends with UX and product development.

How I got here

My Work

UXR | UX | UI | PM | AI

Breaking news credibility assessment for AI & Democracy Hackaton

I was invited to participate in an AI and Democracy hackathon held by the German Marshall Fund and Rockefeller Foundation in November in Mexico City. I contributed as a product designer and product manager on a team with fact-checkers, journalists, tech and AI consultants, and democracy activists. Our team was tasked with developing a service focused on data provenance and source traceability. During an intense one-day hackathon, we developed a proof of concept for a service that can check breaking news trustworthiness in real-time.
